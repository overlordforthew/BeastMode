Beast Mode

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 18, 2026

Beast Mode is a micro-workout and meditation app focused on short daily resets. This policy explains what information Beast Mode stores, how it is used, and how to delete it.

Information Beast Mode stores

  • Account details you provide, such as username and optional email address.
  • Sign-in information, including password hash or Google sign-in linkage if you choose that method.
  • Workout, meditation, streak, mission, and settings data needed to run the app.
  • Push subscription details when you enable nudges on a supported browser or device.

How Beast Mode uses your data

  • To authenticate your account and keep your progress synced.
  • To calculate streaks, points, awards, missions, and leaderboard placement.
  • To send password reset emails and optional push nudges you explicitly enable.
  • To operate and secure the service. Beast Mode does not sell your personal data.

Sharing

Beast Mode does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers needed to operate the app, such as hosting, email delivery, and Google sign-in services when you choose them.

Retention and deletion

You can delete your account inside the app from Settings or request deletion outside the app at /delete-account. Deleting your account removes your profile, settings, streaks, workout history, daily logs, awards, push subscriptions, and password reset records associated with that account.
