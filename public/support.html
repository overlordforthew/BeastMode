Support

Need help with login, nudges, account recovery, or account deletion? This is the public support page for Beast Mode.

Fastest way to get help

Email overlord.gil.ai@gmail.com and include:

  • Your Beast Mode username
  • The device and browser or Android version you are using
  • A short description of the problem
  • A screenshot if the issue is visual or login-related

Account deletion

You can delete your account directly inside the app from Settings. If you no longer have app access, use the instructions at /delete-account.
