Beast Mode

Delete your account

Effective date: April 18, 2026

Beast Mode lets you delete your account inside the app. If you do not have app access anymore, this page explains how to request deletion outside the app.

Delete inside the app

  1. Log in to Beast Mode.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Go to the Danger Zone section.
  4. Type your username and confirm deletion.

Delete outside the app

Email overlord.gil.ai@gmail.com with the subject line Beast Mode Delete Account Request and include:

  • Your Beast Mode username
  • The email address attached to the account, if you added one
  • A short note confirming you want the account permanently deleted

Once processed, Beast Mode deletes your profile, saved settings, workout and meditation history, streak progress, awards, daily logs, push subscriptions, and password reset records tied to that account.
